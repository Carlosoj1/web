What is a good internet user?

A good internet user is someone that knows how to act and work properly in the internet, knowing when to give personal information and in what website, knowing to be kind to others, and without stealing or damaging others image or data. In the next pargraphs we are gonna see how to become a good internet user and how to practice the caracteristics mentioned before.

1. Respect the other users

Behave with etiquette while online. Practice digital etiquette by following the proper standards of conduct and procedure online. This also means to not insultate others, not partcipate in cyberbulling and do not escalate disagreements, we dont know who we are talking to online, so in case of a disagreement we dont know what that person can do, so instead avoid large disagreements.

2. Personal infromation

Exchange information properly. Digital communication, or the electronic exchange of information and correspondence, is the main form of communication between one another onlne. Take care, however, with what you post online. Do not share personal information electronically with people you do not know and on websites you do not trust, instead only give your personal information to websites you can trust in such as bbc.com and only when neccesary.

Conclusion

In conclusion, think twice when talking to others or using not verificated websites, be polite to others and avoid large disagreements, and dont damage or steal others image or data. And for extra tips; Do not download games, music or movies that were made available illegally, do not create malware, dangerous programs, or unsafe websites, do not send spam, dont fall in scams, install an antivirus, and keep your data safe.
